# SEO next steps for aicuatoi.net

## Da lam trong code

- Google Search Console verification meta tag da duoc them vao `app/Views/layout.php`.
- Sitemap da co san pham, danh muc, blog, trang san pham tong va bo qua san pham hidden.
- Canonical da dung hon cho danh muc va trang phan trang.
- Search/sort duoc gan `noindex,follow` de tranh tao nhieu trang index mong.
- Product JSON-LD dung gia theo goi/bien the, co availability, rating, FAQ va breadcrumb schema.
- Trang danh muc/san pham tong co ItemList schema.
- Anh san pham co alt theo ten san pham.
- Anh quan trong co `fetchpriority="high"`, anh phu co `loading="lazy"` va `decoding="async"`.

## Can lam sau khi deploy

1. Vao Google Search Console, verify domain bang meta tag da them.
2. Submit sitemap: `https://aicuatoi.net/sitemap.xml`.
3. Dung URL Inspection request index cac trang san pham chinh.
4. Chay Rich Results Test cho 3-5 trang san pham de kiem tra Product/FAQ/Breadcrumb schema.
5. Chay PageSpeed Insights tren mobile cho trang chu, danh muc hot va trang chi tiet san pham hot.
6. Nen anh san pham sang WebP, uu tien anh tren 200 KB.
7. Viet noi dung chi tiet rieng cho tung san pham, khong copy/paste y chang nhau.
8. Viet blog theo cum tu khoa va link ve san pham lien quan.
9. Them review that tu khach hang neu muon rich result rating ben vung hon.

## Mau noi dung san pham nen co

- Gioi thieu san pham va doi tuong phu hop.
- Goi nay bao gom nhung gi.
- Loi ich va tinh nang noi bat.
- Cach mua va cach nhan hang.
- Chinh sach bao hanh.
- FAQ rieng cua san pham.
